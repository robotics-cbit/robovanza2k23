About ROBOVANZA

ROBOTICS AND INNOVATION CLUB of CBIT has come to bring out the best of its technicalities and innovation. We present you a bigger and a better version of our event “ ROBOVANZA-2K23” conducted at CBIT. This fest emphasizes to encourage all the enthusiasts who want to seek knowledge in the art of ROBOTICS. The members of our club have been striving to be the best in this ART while polishing their skills everywhere possible. Here, we provide the best platform to showcase their talents. ROBOTICS requires high level of intellect and thinking, and this is what the members of our club posses. We wish to broaden our horizons through ROBOVANZA where we believe :-

“Knowledge when shared, becomes Wisdom”
So we wish to see you all on 31st of January and 1st of February 2023
Come explore and share.

ROBO SOCCER

Set your goals at the goal post.

All you soccer fans hold your breath, as we present to you soccer with an entirely new dimension with ROBO SOCCER. Get your soccer playing bots ready to compete on the soccer arena and bring out the hidden RONALDO,MESSI in your bots.



GRAND PRIX NITRO

DRONE RACING

Float like a Cadillac, sting like a Beemer.

Drone racing....the word drone reminds the sound of bee "buzz", push the throttle stick all the way up and pitch forward into the competition to put yourself in the fastest lap possible around the track.



LINE FOLLOWER

Finish the line to shine.

Line follower....tracking the line is easy but it's gets tricky when junctions confuse the bots to take a wrong route. So tell your bot to follow the best line to complete the path in fastest time!
