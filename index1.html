About ROBOVANZA

ROBOTICS AND INNOVATION CLUB of CBIT has come to bring out the best of its technicalities and innovation. We present you a bigger and a better version of our event “ ROBOVANZA-2K23” conducted at CBIT. This fest emphasizes to encourage all the enthusiasts who want to seek knowledge in the art of ROBOTICS. The members of our club have been striving to be the best in this ART while polishing their skills everywhere possible. Here, we provide the best platform to showcase their talents. ROBOTICS requires high level of intellect and thinking, and this is what the members of our club posses. We wish to broaden our horizons through ROBOVANZA where we believe :-

“Knowledge when shared, becomes Wisdom”
So we wish to see you all on 31st of January and 1st of Febraury 2023
Come explore and share.

Events

Join us for unforgettable thrill of robotics and ameliorate your talent in ROBOVANZA 2K23,
The robotics fest of CBIT.
To all those who are awaiting to join us, tickets are now available.



BLAST IT

Pop the baloons to conquer.

How will robots transform human warfare? To find answers, Build your own robot that can pop the baloons of other opponents and become a conquerer.



ROBO RACE

Beat the deadline, to be the champion.

Anchorage this to put your problem solving skills in test and create a functioning robot within 24 hours that can help resolve the given real world problem, working anywhere you wish. The best robot and effective solution shall triumph!


ALL-TERRAIN ROBOT

Lead your robot where no robot has ever gone before.

Build your bot robust enough to maneuver over a challenging track of unfriendly rocks,flat tiled floor or sloping surfaces. Get your wheels dusty that tackles with fresh impetus and show your best off-road performance.


TUG OF WAR

For the strongest of the strong.

Imagine the adrenaline rush of fighting in a tug of war. Exciting isn't it? Now think what it would be like if robots played the sport. Tug of War is for the strongest among the strong. You are required to build a robot which will pull so hard that your opponent decides to let go their hope of winning. Think you're up for the challenge? Start building your Tug of War robot now!


ROBO SOCCER

Set your goals at the goal post.

Wanna feel like Messi? then your at the right event. Build a bot and play soccer. Not just handling the ball, but tackle the opponent’s defender bot and make a goal . ‘To become successful set your goals' at the goal post!


ROBO SEESAW

Falling is the first step to success doesn't work always.

Crossing bridges is always fun...why not make your bot do it this time? The bridge is surrounded by pits on either side with challenges filled. Let your bot complete this adventure itself. Remember ‘falling is the first step to success ‘ doesn’t work here!


LINE FOLLOWER

Finish the line to shine.

Create your bot and let it follow a black line on a white surface. Sounds simple? Then think again, because only the smartest can survive the complex tracks filled with crazy twists and turns.


ROBO MAZE

Solve the maze.

Robo Maze is one of the simple looking, yet tough to crack events in Robovanza. An autonomous robot has to complete a tricky maze in the shortest possible time. The quickest one to finish the maze wins.
